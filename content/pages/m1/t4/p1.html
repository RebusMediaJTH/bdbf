Most people we know are on social media these days, and if that includes you, you need to recognise how using social media in your personal time can impact your employment with Australia Post. This might include posting about your work or the people you work with, or commenting on posts related to your work.

When your personal social media use intersects with your work:

  • treat others with courtesy and respect
  • take responsibility for what you post, and
  • ensure any statements you make do not negatively affect our brand, reputation, commercial interests or customer confidence.
