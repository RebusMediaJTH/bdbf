It's the same at work

Those lessons we learnt when we were younger haven't changed. We know treating others with disrespect causes:

  • Relationships at work to sour
  • People's quality and output of work to suffer
  • Safety issues because people don't have their mind on what they're doing
  • Stress at work from potential legal issues.
Yep - 'Treat others the way you'd like to be treated' — I apply that rule in all situations, in my personal life and when dealing with people at work. It's a simple rule but it helps me make better decisions.

What kinds of behaviours should we be on the look-out for?

You know what disrespectful and impolite behaviour is, because you've probably been on the receiving end of it at some point in your life, right?

So look-out for this type of behaviour in your daily interactions with work mates as well as in any communication technology (including social media).

It can occur between:

  • individuals or groups
  • co-workers
  • People Leaders and their team members (in either direction)
  • our staff and the general public.
