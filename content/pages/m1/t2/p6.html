Check your understanding

Vanessa is a truck driver with StarTrack. She has recently injured her back and has been prescribed pain medication by her doctor.

Vanessa says:

"I drive a truck in my job. Is there anything I need to know about this medication?"

The Doctor says:

"This medication is known to impair physical and mental performance. You should not take it before operating a vehicle or any machinery."

How should Vanessa handle this situation?

Select the best answer.

  • Vanessa should use energy drinks and coffee to keep her alert whilst driving under the influence of her pain medication.
  • Vanessa should discuss this issue with her People Leader before her next shift.
  • The warnings on pain medications are recommendations only. Vanessa should carry on with her job as normal and reassess the situation if she experiences drowsiness while driving.

Correct.

As a driver, Vanessa's work would create a high risk of causing harm were she to drive under the influence of her pain medications.

By discussing the issue with her People Leader they can work out a way to get her back to work as soon as possible without creating a risk to safety.
