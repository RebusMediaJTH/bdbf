The communities we serve are our communities

The decisions we make have the potential to cause harm when we:

  • Consume alcohol and other drugs, including over-the-counter and prescription medications
  • Provide financial services
  • Freight dangerous goods
  • Interact with members of the community
  • Operate vehicles and machinery, and carry out other work in public spaces
