It's the same for all of us at work

Our people are the heart of our business and we're dedicated to ensuring you have the skills and knowledge required to get the job done the right way, every time.

Some roles require specialised training to ensure we are operating lawfully. They include roles in which our people are:

  • Providing financial services
  • Handling dangerous goods
  • Ensuring responsible management and organisational competence
The training I receive is important to me because it keeps everyone in my business safe and also protects the community.

Some responsibilities are shared by all of us...

There are three important areas for which we all receive training that helps us make better decisions in our interactions with people at work:

  • Harassment, Discrimination and Bullying
  • Social Media
  • Work Health and Safety.
As a People Leader I'm trained to empower my team and support them when issues arise.

We're always improving through regular training and learning events.

We have many policies to help you make better decisions at work. Should you want to know more about how we ensure you have the skills and knowledge you require in performing your role, take a look at the core policy overviews at the end of this module.
