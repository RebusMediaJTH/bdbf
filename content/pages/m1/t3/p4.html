As a group, we all need to work together and pull in the same direction so that we all benefit.

Part of this mindset means being aware of the ways in which we can damage APG such as:

  • Posting or sharing damaging content on social media
  • Not using business resources properly, or fraudulently
  • Acting in ways which are against Our Ethics and the law
APG trusts me to work within the guidelines to help deliver a better future for our business and all of our stakeholders.

Our success is shared

We provide clear guidelines to help you make decisions that prevent harm occurring to our business and to our brand.

Need to report unlawful or improper conduct?

You can speak to your People Leader or you can call the Whistleblower Hotline on 1800 799 353.

We'll document your complaint and assess it.

You'll be protected

If you report something to the Whistleblower Hotline number, this is what you can expect FROM US:

  • You can remain anonymous.
  • We'll protect your identity as much as we can and to the extent we can legally do so.
  • We'll protect you from reprisals.

This is what we expect FROM YOU:

  • Your complaint is truthful.
  • You're not simply trying to get someone into trouble for no good reason. (Obviously, there'll be disciplinary action if this is the case.)
