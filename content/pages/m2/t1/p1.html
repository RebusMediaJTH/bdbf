It's the same for our customers

The nature of the work we do requires that we consider a range of issues that are very important to our customers. These include:

  • Our customers' privacy (and in particular the way we handle Personal Information like credit card numbers and expiry dates)
  • How we handle customer complaints and disputes

"If it's important to our customers, it's important to us."

Placing my customers at the centre of what I do reminds me of what's important and helps me make better decisions.

We're always asking ourselves:

How does what I do affect our customers?
