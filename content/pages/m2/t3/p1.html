We protect our customers' Personal Information and its use

Our size and the nature of the work we do mean we handle more Personal Information than most large companies in Australia.

Our customers trust us with their Personal Information when they:

  • Send or receive a letter or parcel
  • Apply for a passport
  • Register to use our online services
  • Make a payment to us using a payment card (eg Credit Cards and EFTPOS)

There are very specific guidelines that need to be followed when handling Personal Information. We'll cover these shortly.
