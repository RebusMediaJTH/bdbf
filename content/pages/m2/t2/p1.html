This is what 'delighting our customers' is all about...

Key to our future success is delivering experiences that our customers value, that set us apart from competitors, and make customers our greatest advocates.

It's our people who make us who we are and create the customer experiences that matter most.

Our Customer Commitment describes the level of service customers can expect from Australia Post.

Our Customer Commitment is:

We have an unwavering commitment to delivering services that give you more convenience, control and choice.

We will strive to:

  1. Help you connect to others and the world – your way.
  2. Listen and respond to what you tell us.
  3. Provide you with products and services that you want and need.
  4. Make it easier to get what you need, when you need it.
  5. Meet your future needs.
  6. Do more than you expect.

What matters to you, matters to us.

We provide great customer experiences and we celebrate our people's successes in delighting our customers.

Of course, we're always looking to improve our customer experiences and to consistently delight them. This means providing:

  • Innovative and easy to use products and services
  • Friendly service by knowledgeable staff
  • Consistent on time delivery
  • Value for money
  • Modern, efficient networks

We will build our people's commitment to these goals by:

  • Promoting job satisfaction
  • Encouraging participation in the success of the business
  • Recognising outstanding service to customers
  • Rewarding outstanding performance

What does quality mean to you?

It’s pretty simple – no matter what work you do, always think about the customer. Ask yourself:

What’s going to delight them?
How is what I'm doing going to help them?
