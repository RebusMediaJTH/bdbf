Helping us make better decisions

This course is about making better decisions when interacting with our people, customers, and partners. It's also about making the right choices within our business, as well as when you're working within the community and environment.

So it only seems logical that we divide our policies into these categories below.

Our People

Our people are the heart of our business. We work in teams that are as diverse as the communities in which we operate.

Working together means helping each other, respecting each other and communicating with each other to provide the best possible outcomes for our customers, our partners and the community, and to ensure a safe, healthy and productive workplace.

Our Customers

Our customers rely on us every day in so many ways. We take this responsibility seriously because our customers are the key to our future success.

We take pride in delighting our customers because we understand the hard work that goes into providing them with our best possible service and in doing our utmost to protect their interests.

Our Community and the Environment

Our broader Australian community and the land in which we live are two things we all share in common. We have a responsibility to act in a sustainable manner which serves the best interests of both.

To achieve this, we work with communities and other stakeholders around the country to ensure the changes we make today will result in a better tomorrow for all.

Our Business

Our systems and processes ensure we work in a way that's consistent with our values, community expectations and government legislation.

They are designed to help us be at our best today, and to give us the flexibility to improve for tomorrow, in all of our interactions.

Our Partners

Our partners rely on us and we rely on them.

Working together with businesses and organisations of all kinds around Australia is a vital part of what we do.

We are continually striving to further strengthen and improve these relationships, all of which are built on trust and respect.
