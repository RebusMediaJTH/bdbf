Who are our partners?

Business partners

We work with all kinds of businesses, contractors, and vendors across Australia to ensure we're able to meet the ever-changing needs of our business, our people, our customers and the community.

Regulatory bodies and government agencies

We work with regulatory bodies and government agencies to make sure we're compliant with all laws and regulations. Business activities covered include:

  • Financial services
  • Transport and logistics
  • Human resources
  • Environment and sustainability

Interest groups

We work with interest groups to ensure we're making a positive impact on:

  • Our communities
  • Consumers
  • The environment
We value our partners because they're a part of our team... we couldn't do what we do without them.

We have certain responsibilities when engaging our partners

  • We identify conflict of interest situations and act appropriately
  • We act according to community and corporate standards
  • We always act within the law and follow policies
  • Our decision-making is:
    • Fair
    • Impartial
    • Objective
  • Our decision-making is free from:
    • Competing self-interest
    • Prejudice
    • Favouritism
    • Influence by the offering of gifts, benefits and hospitality

Which of our partners do you interact with at work?

You may not realise it, but almost all of our people are involved in some way in our partnership with the Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA).

Wondering how that works?

We partner with CASA to ensure that every parcel we put on an airplane is safe and does not pose a risk of harm for our people or our community. We do this by working together to ensure our work practices meet the safety standards required by law.

So if you're part of the process of placing parcels or letters that are flown domestically or internationally, you're involved in the work we do as a team and in partnership with CASA.
