Our partners expect the same from us

When working with our partners, we need to make sure that any of our relationships, activities or interests outside of APG don't influence or might be seen by others to influence any decisions that we make. These are called 'conflicts of interest'.

Conflicts of interest can also occur when working with partners when:

  • You, or a family member or close personal friend holds a direct interest in a business or organisation we partner with, and
  • You stand to generate a personal gain for yourself or a family member, or a close personal friend.

We need to avoid conflicts of interest and receiving or giving gifts, benefits and hospitality that can affect our decision-making or the way others might perceive our decision-making.

In line with Our Ethics and Our Values, we act honestly and with integrity when WORKING with our partners.

We have many policies to help you make better decisions within our partnerships. Should you want to know more about how we do the right thing in our dealings with our partners, take a look at the core policy overviews at the end of this module.
