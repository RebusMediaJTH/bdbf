Your responsibilities

Everyone who works for or represents our business has the following responsibilities:

Avoid these situations

  • Situations or actions that create, or may be seen to create a conflict of interest.
  • Situations that may interfere with your ability to make good judgements.

Avoid these decisions

Avoid making decisions where you may be seen to:

  • Personally make a gain.
  • Make a gain for a family member or a close personal friend.

Tell someone

  • Inform a manager, supervisor or another appropriate person where a conflict of interest actually exists or may be seen to be the case by others.

What you need to do

Everyone who works for or represents our business needs to avoid any business interest, outside activity or relationship, financial or otherwise, which might:

  • Affect or influence sound judgement
  • Affect performance of duties or services
  • Affect our brand or our business's reputation
