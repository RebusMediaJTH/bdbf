We have four simple rules

Report it

All gifts, benefits and hospitalities worth $250 or more offered to you or by you, must be recorded in the Gifts, Benefits and Hospitality Register (Australia Post) or a Gifts, Benefits and Hospitality Form completed (subsidiaries), regardless of whether they're accepted or declined.

You must also report any repeated offers from or to the same party in the Gifts, Benefits and Hospitality Register, regardless of value.

No influence

Gifts, hospitality or entertainment mustn't influence or be seen to influence business decisions.

No cash

No cash or cash-like gifts can be accepted or offered.

Good judgement

Use good judgement. If you think a gift, hospitality or other benefit could influence, or be perceived to influence a decision or cause favouritism, don't accept or offer it.
