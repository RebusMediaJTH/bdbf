It's the same in the partnerships we form at work

Our brand is built on the trust of our customers, our community and our partners.

We work to protect our brand and maintain that trust by:

  • Protecting the intellectual property rights of our business and those of our partners (e.g. trademarks, copyrights, and "trade secrets").
  • Making sure that our procurement is done honestly, transparently and according to process.
  • Working with government regulators and agencies, and keeping detailed records of our meetings.
  • Making sure any gifts, benefits or hospitality doesn't or can't be seen to influence our decisions.
We're all responsible for protecting our brand and maintaining the trust of our customers, the community and our partners.

We protect our assets

When working with our partners we protect our assets. These include:

  • Any financial investments we make such as the resources we use at work.
  • Any intellectual investments we make such as research into innovative, new products or ways of providing better service.

Even our brand and reputation are assets that need to be protected.

We do this by:

  • Looking after all of the resources provided by APG.
  • Keeping any intellectual property confidential.
  • Doing the right things in our relationships with regulators.
  • Responding promptly to any government agency requests.
