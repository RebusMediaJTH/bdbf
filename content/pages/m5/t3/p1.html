We all contribute to the safety of aviation transport

If you've ever handled a parcel or letter that was flown domestically or internationally, you've been involved in the work we do to ensure the safety of aviation transport.

  • We have processes in place that we follow when handling air mail and air freight.
  • We provide training so everyone understands our processes and are able to make the right decisions.
  • We have checks and balances in place to make sure we're acting within the law.
  • We regularly report how we're acting in-line with the Aviation Transport Security Act.

Our safety culture extends across our entire organisation; everyone is involved.

We have many policies to help you make better decisions within our business. Should you want to learn more about our safety culture, take a look at the core policy overviews at the end of this module.
