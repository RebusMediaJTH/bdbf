It's the same for us at work

We manage our business's finances responsibly so we're able to continue operating sustainably into the future.

Our People Leaders, Managers and Executives have a responsibility to work within the scope of their delegated authority.

Delegations relate to business activities such as:

  • Customer revenue contracts
  • Procurement and expenditure
  • Other contracts
  • Settlement of disputes
  • Corporate functions, including:
    • The signing of employment contracts
    • Make decisions regarding workers
    • The approval of certain financial transactions or entries (such as writing off a bad debt or writing down the value of stock for Retail Outlets)

If you're making decisions that impact our business's financial position, consideration should be given to the following questions:

Am I following the correct process in making this decision?
Does this decision represent good value for my business?
