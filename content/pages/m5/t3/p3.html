Our processes help us make the right decisions

A lot of the work we do is shaped by processes that help our people make the right decisions. This provides consistency and accountability across our business.

Generally these processes are risk-based and are designed to:

  • Manage risk responsibly
  • Safely transport goods by air
  • Allocate and spend funds responsibly
  • Handle digital information securely
  • Develop products that are great for our customers and good for our business
  • Carefully monitor, track, report on and close our projects
  • Adhere to our governance framework
  • Manage our tax responsibilities effectively
Risk management is about identifying risks and working out how to minimise or manage them should they occur.

Personal Vehicle

  • Regular servicing (e.g. oil change, new tires)

  • Change the windscreen wipers

Home

  • Regular cleaning and/or maintenance
  • Check batteries in fire alarms
  • Lock external doors when not home
  • Buy insurance

Travel

  • Leave details of your travels with a friend or relative
  • Take photocopies of your passport
  • Book through reputable agents/websites
  • Buy insurance

Technology

  • Password protect your phone/tablet/computer
  • Regularly change your passwords
  • Make backups of your photos and/or other important data
  • Set reminders to help prevent exceeding your data allowance
