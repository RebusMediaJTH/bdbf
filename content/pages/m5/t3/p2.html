What's an 'incident'?

At APG, an 'incident' isn't just a safety issue that causes an injury, damage or a near miss. It's any instance or result which may have serious consequences. For example, it could be related to somebody not protecting customer privacy in-line with our policies and the law, a breach of our IT network, or incidents of fraud and corruption.

Examples of incidents include...

  • somebody hacking into our computer systems
  • fake transactions being entered
  • customers who raise your concern due to questions asked or information provided
  • personal information that may be compromised
  • security breaches
  • environmental spills

Of course, these are just a few examples. There are many more.

If you identify an incident at work, you must log it as soon as practicable.
