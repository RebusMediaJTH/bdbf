We all all need to prevent fraud and work within all laws and regulations.

The actions of one person or a few can impact all of us, our business, our customers, and the community. This is why we're committed to:

  • preventing
  • detecting, and
  • investigating

any reports of fraud or corruption.

We don't make, and won't accept, facilitation payments or 'kickbacks'.

We always minimise any possibility of fraud and corruption activity...

... by completing risk assessments for all major business initiatives, projects, business as usual activities, and significant changes. We also highlight any areas that may be exposed to fraud and corruption in a risk register or fraud management plan.

Think about it this way...

If you're found to be committing fraud or corruption, it could lead to:

  • recouping any damage or loss
  • legal prosecution
  • as well as our own disciplinary process.
