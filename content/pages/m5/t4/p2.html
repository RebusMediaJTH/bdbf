We communicate information appropriately when required

We maintain our trust in our business practices by promptly and transparently communicating information. This is necessary to ensure we're:

  • Maintaining an open and honest relationship with the Australian Taxation Office
  • Keeping key stakeholders informed during our incident management process
  • Keeping key stakeholders informed on risks in regards:
    • to our financial position
    • to our investment portfolio
    • to information security across our IT infrastructure.
