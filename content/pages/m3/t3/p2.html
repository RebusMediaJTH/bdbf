We work with integrity, honesty and fairness

The decisions we make in our interactions with the world around us can have impacts for generations to come.

In line with Our Values, Our Ethics and our legal responsibilities we work to ensure the Australian public has appropriate access to our record on matters that affect the community and the environment. These include our performance in:

  • Managing the safety of our road transport supply chain
  • Protecting the environment
  • Managing changes to our business in-line with our Customer Service Obligations
We're not transparent just because the law requires it; we want to continue to work with our community and people with integrity, honesty and fairness - that's what Our Ethics and Our Values are about!

We have many policies to help you make better decisions to protect our community and the environment. Should you want to know more about how we remain accountable, take a look at the core policy overviews at the end of this module.
