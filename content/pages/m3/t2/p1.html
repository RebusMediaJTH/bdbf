Let's talk about social issues and how we impact them...

...Because we're always improving

Living Our Values and delivering a better future for everyone, everywhere, every day is only possible when we engage with the community and our stakeholders on the issues that affect us all.

It doesn't matter where they live, Australians can rely on us to deliver to everyone, everywhere.

Our communities rely on us to respond to their changing needs

Some of the improvements we make are self-driven. Some of them are suggested by the communities we serve.

When a change to how we deliver is requested by a community we get in there and ensure the change is in the best interests of the majority of people in that community.

We do this by conducting surveys to measure actual support for proposed improvements.
