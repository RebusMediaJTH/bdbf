It's the same at work

We're committed to a culture focused on the environment including:

  • Becoming more fuel and energy efficient
  • Reducing pollution and waste
  • Minimising or eliminating environmental risks
  • Considering the environment in our strategies, decisions, products and services
  • Engaging with the community on environmental issues
  • Transparently reporting on our environmental performance
  • Working within the law and regulations.
When it comes to the environment, we're always looking at how we can be more efficient and reduce waste.

How can we all get involved?

All the little things we do add up to make a big difference. Which of the following can you get involved in both at home and at work?

Tick all of the actions relevant to you. Select Submit when you are done.

  • Sorting waste into recycling bins appropriately
  • Finding ways to safely reuse waste products
  • Printing double-sided whenever practical
  • Minimising water use
  • Using the half flush on the toilet when appropriate
  • Fixing leaks
  • Replacing old lights with energy efficient alternatives
  • Turning lights off in rooms that aren't being used (if safe to do so)
  • Keeping windows and doors closed that help to minimise energy used for heating and cooling
  • Operating vehicles without unnecessarily excessive acceleration and braking
  • Ensuring vehicles are regularly maintained
