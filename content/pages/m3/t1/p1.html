Everything we do is about creating a better future for everyone, everywhere, every day.

Our success is directly linked to the success of our communities so we're always trying to create 'shared value' for both our business and for the communities we serve.

We play our part in protecting the community

Driving in our vans and trucks every day, means we have a duty to take care on our roads.

That responsibility doesn't just fall on our drivers; it belongs to everyone involved in the safe movement of mail and parcels, from pickup to the delivery point.

We keep our communities and our people safe by:

  • Managing driver fatigue
  • Securing loads safely
  • Providing the right type of training to everybody working in the supply chain
Keeping the roads safe is a team effort - it includes Managers, Sales, Consignors, Loaders, Packers, Drivers and Consignees. It's called the Chain of Responsibility and everyone plays a role.
