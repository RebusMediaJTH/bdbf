Who are our stakeholders?

Being such a large organisation, with so many different roles and business areas covering all of Australia means almost everyone's a stakeholder.

Our stakeholders include...

  • You and all of our other workers
  • Our customers
  • Communities all around Australia
  • Our business partners
  • Our shareholder
  • State and local governments
