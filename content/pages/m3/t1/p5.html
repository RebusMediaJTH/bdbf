It's the same at work

Being proactive and communicating about risks is a responsibility we all share, no matter what your role is.

This doesn't just apply to your workplace, you should also be on the look out in the community, especially when it comes to road safety and hazards in the environment.

Our community relies on us to help keep the roads as safe as possible... we work as a team to control the risks.
If you see something unsafe on the road or something which may be a hazard to the environment, report it to your People Leader immediately.
