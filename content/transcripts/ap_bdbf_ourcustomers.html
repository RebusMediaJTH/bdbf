Our customers are at the centre of everything we do.

...adapting to a changing world, always improving, and meeting our customers' ever-changing needs.

Our business is built on the trust of our customers.

Our customers trust us to provide quality products and services.

They trust us to protect their privacy and all of their Personal Information we handle.

They trust us to compete fairly in the marketplace and to treat consumers with courtesy and respect.

If you're unable to access the Corporate Intranet, talk to your People Leader.
