With the amount of airline passengers and cargo increasing year on year, security incidents sometimes make the headlines.

At Australia Post Group, we are responsible for billions of items each year using sea, road and air transport.

It's our job to ensure that this mail is kept safe and secure so that people, their property and the environment stay safe. But what does this have to do with you?

Well, no matter where you work in the Australia Post Group, you need to be alert each and every day for any suspect items in the Cargo stream, like dangerous goods, or improvised explosive devices (also called IEDs), and deal with them properly if you find them.

In this course, we'll teach you what to do — so you can help to keep our Air Cargo Safe and Secure.
